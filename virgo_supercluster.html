The Virgo Supercluster

The Virgo Supercluster or the Local Supercluster is a mass concentration of galaxies containing the Virgo Cluster and Local Group, which in turn contains the Milky Way and Andromeda galaxies.

At least 100 galaxy groups and clusters are located within its diameter of 33 megaparsecs (110 million light-years).
